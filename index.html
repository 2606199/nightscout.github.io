Nightscout aka, CGM in the Cloud

We are not waiting to put our CGMs in the cloud.

This is a website run by a community of DIYers to put their Dexcom CGM in the cloud.

  • Highly experimental.
  • Not intended for therapy.
  • Intended only as an investigational and educational tool to learn about this technology.
